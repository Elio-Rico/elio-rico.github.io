About me

I am a third year PhD Student at the University of Lausanne. My research interests include but are not restricted to Financial Stability, Monetary Policy, Capital Flows and Information Frictions.
Besides that, I am passioned about new methods to make use of Big Data and its visualization to exploit the enormous amount of information that we have access to nowadays.
